 1.Initialization
    i] socket and chess object are initialized
    ii] Board element is selected from the DOM
    iii] initial values for draggedpiece , sourcesquare, and playerRole are set to null.

2.initial Board Rendering 
    i] renderboard() is called  to display the initial state for chessboard.

3.Drag and Drop Functionlity
i] renderboard set-up drag-and-drop event listeners  for each piece and sqaure (manje jeva aapn ekda piace move karnar teva te sagal detect honar )
ii] pieces are draggable based on the player role
iii] when piece is dragged , draggedpiece and sourcesquare are set
iv] when a piece is dropped , handlemove() is called to handle the move logic and emit it to the server
